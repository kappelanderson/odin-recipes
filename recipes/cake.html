CHOCOLATE CAKE

Description

This high protein chocolate cake has super nutritional values. The perfect fitness cake with protein powder.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups White whole wheat flour (240g)
  • 2 Cups Granulated sugar
  • 2/3 Cup Cocoa powder (sifted if lumpy 60g)
  • 2 tsp Baking powder
  • 3/4 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Baking soda
  • 1 Cup Milk, of choice at room temperature *important*
  • 2/3 Cup Plain, non-fat Greek yogurt, at room temperature *important*
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 tsp Vanilla extract
  • 1/2 Cup Very hot water
  • 3/4 Cup Unsalted butter
  • 1/2 Cup Plain, non-fat Greek yogurt
  • 1 Cups Powdered sugar (220g)
  • 1/4 Cup Cocoa powder (25g)
  • 2 tsp Vanilla
  • 1/4 tsp Salt

Steps

  1. Pre heat your oven to 350 degrees F and line the bottom of 2 8 inch cake pans with parchment paper, spraying the sides with cooking spray.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk all the ingredients up to the milk.
  3. Add all the remaining ingredients, except the water, and whisk just a little bit until it just starts to mix together but is not fully mixed.
  4. Pour the hot water over and whisk until smooth.
  5. Divide between the 2 pans and bake until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean, about 38-42 minutes. Let cool in the pan completely.
  6. While the cake is cooling, beat the butter and Greek yogurt in a large bowl until fluffy.
  7. Gradually add in then powdered sugar and cocoa powder and beat until mixed.
  8. Beat in the vanilla and salt.
  9. Transfer to a bowl, cover and refrigerate to firm up while the cake cools.
  10. Once the cake is cool, place one of the cake layers on a cake stand, bottom side up and spread 3/4 cup of the frosting on top.
  11. Place the other cake on top. Spread a VERY thin layer of frosting over the cake to seal in the crumbs, then chill 30 minutes.
  12. Once chilled, frost the rest of the cake
  13. DEVOUR
