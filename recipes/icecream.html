Chocolate Ice Cream

Description

Rich and creamy homemade chocolate protein ice cream that’s secretly GOOD for you at the same time!

Ingredients

  • 4 frozen overripe bananas (banana-free options are listed below)
  • 1/4 cup almond butter, or allergy friendly sub
  • 3 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 2 tbsp chocolate protein powder
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • optional 1/4 tsp instant coffee

Instructions

Blend all ingredients until completely smooth. (If you’re not using a Vitamix or food processor, thaw the bananas a little before blending so your machine will be able to handle them.) Either serve immediately as soft serve, or freeze up to an hour and then scoop out with an ice cream scoop for the classic ice-cream shape.
